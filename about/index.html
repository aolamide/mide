About Me

I'm a product designer working on Hexapay at Bithyve. I’m an undergraduate in the Electrical and Electronics program at the University of Lagos. In the past, I've worked with companies such as AIESEC and Newport Global Solutions.

I’ve always loved the engineering part of life, but quickly realized that I was fascinated by the 'Why' behind the code I was writing. This led me to exploring the field of UX, and after a few side projects and freelance work, I fell in love with the art of problem solving.

I like to think of myself as a problem solver who can lead a project from research & design to the prototyping phase to ensure a smooth developer handoff.
